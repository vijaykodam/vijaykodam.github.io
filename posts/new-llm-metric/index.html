New metric to measure the efficiency of AI models

January 28, 2025 1-minute read
Cloud • GenAI • LLM
GenAI • Deepseek • LLM

At the end of the day, how cheaply and energy efficiently can you generate tokens matters.

“Tokens per watt per dollar” metric mentioned by Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, is trying to achieve the same.

This will be a metric by which every LLM is going to be benchmarked against. This is the metric which will showcase how long can an AI investment lasts and how much is your profitability of AI startups.

There will be a comparison of LLMs with “Tokens per watt per dollar” soon, if not already existing.
