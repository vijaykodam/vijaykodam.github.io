Fitness Calculator

The 2 Km Walk Test is a reliable way to estimate your maximal aerobic power (VO2max) and assess your fitness level.

To ensure accurate results, please follow these steps before filling in the details below:

  1. Preparation: Ensure you are in good health and fit to undertake the test. Wear comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for walking.
  2. Walk 2 Kilometers: Find a flat, measured course (e.g., a track or a known distance on a path). Walk a total distance of 2 kilometers at a brisk, steady pace.
  3. Collect Your Data: As soon as you finish the 2 km walk, immediately measure your heart rate (beats per minute) and note the total time taken to complete the walk (in minutes and seconds).
  4. Enter Your Details: Once you have completed the walk and collected the necessary data, fill in the information below to calculate your fitness index.
