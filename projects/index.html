Projects

Welcome to my collection of experimental projects powered by Large Language Models (LLMs). My goal is to develop end-to-end solutions that are both practical and personally useful, with the assistance of LLMs. Each project here is a client-side Single Page Application (SPA), built primarily using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These projects are designed to be lightweight, minimal, and fully functional without the need for a server backend—ensuring they can run indefinitely without incurring additional costs.

I invite you to explore each project. Your feedback is highly valued, and you can connect with me on X (Twitter) or LinkedIn to share your thoughts.
